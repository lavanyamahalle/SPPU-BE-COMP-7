### **Q1a) Note on Bitcoin [9]**

**Bitcoin** is the first and most widely recognized cryptocurrency, introduced in **2008 by an anonymous person or group named Satoshi Nakamoto**. It is a **decentralized digital currency** that allows peer-to-peer transactions without the need for intermediaries like banks.

**Key Features:**

1. **Decentralized** ‚Äì No central authority controls it; transactions are validated by a network of nodes using blockchain technology.
2. **Limited Supply** ‚Äì Maximum of 21 million bitcoins can ever exist, which prevents inflation.
3. **Blockchain** ‚Äì A public ledger that records all Bitcoin transactions in a secure and transparent way.
4. **Pseudonymous Transactions** ‚Äì Users are identified by addresses, not personal information.
5. **Mining** ‚Äì Process of validating transactions and adding them to the blockchain in exchange for new bitcoins.

**Advantages:**

* Fast international transactions.
* Lower transaction fees than banks.
* Transparent and secure due to blockchain technology.

**Limitations:**

* High price volatility.
* Not widely accepted everywhere.
* Energy-intensive mining process.
